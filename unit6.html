Introduction

Objective: By the end of this unit, learners will understand how to form and use negative statements in the present simple tense.

Overview: This unit covers the formation and usage of negative statements in the present simple tense.

Explanation

Forming Negative Statements: To form negative statements in the present simple tense, use "do not" (don't) or "does not" (doesn't) followed by the base form of the verb.

  • I/You/We/They do not (don't) work
  • He/She/It does not (doesn't) work

Examples

Negative Statements:

  • I do not (don't) play the piano.
  • You do not (don't) like coffee.
  • He/She/It does not (doesn't) go to school.
  • We do not (don't) watch TV.
  • They do not (don't) travel often.

Practice Exercises

Exercise 6.1: Rewrite the sentences in the negative form:

  • 1. I play the guitar well. → I do not (don't) play the guitar well.
  • 2. Jane writes poetry beautifully. → Jane does not (doesn't) write poetry beautifully.
  • 3. They know my address. → They do not (don't) know my address.
  • 4. We work on weekends. → We do not (don't) work on weekends.
  • 5. He has breakfast at 7 AM. → He does not (doesn't) have breakfast at 7 AM.
  • 6. You do your homework every day. → You do not (don't) do your homework every day.

Exercise 6.2: Complete the sentences with "do not" or "does not":

  • 1. I ___ (do not) like horror movies.
  • 2. She ___ (does not) eat meat.
  • 3. They ___ (do not) play soccer.
  • 4. He ___ (does not) live here.
  • 5. We ___ (do not) go to the gym on Sundays.
  • 6. You ___ (do not) work on holidays.

Exercise 6.3: Make negative sentences about the people:

  • 1. (Susan / like / pizza) Susan does not (doesn't) like pizza.
  • 2. (Tom / watch / TV) Tom does not (doesn't) watch TV.
  • 3. (We / study / French) We do not (don't) study French.
  • 4. (They / play / golf) They do not (don't) play golf.
  • 5. (You / read / books) You do not (don't) read books.
  • 6. (I / drink / coffee) I do not (don't) drink coffee.

Exercise 6.4: Use the given prompts to create negative statements:

  • 1. (she / drive / to work) She does not (doesn't) drive to work.
  • 2. (we / eat / fast food) We do not (don't) eat fast food.
  • 3. (he / exercise / regularly) He does not (doesn't) exercise regularly.
  • 4. (they / go / to the beach) They do not (don't) go to the beach.
  • 5. (you / visit / the museum) You do not (don't) visit the museum.

Exercise 6.5: Correct the mistakes in the negative sentences:

  • 1. She don't like ice cream. → She does not (doesn't) like ice cream.
  • 2. We doesn't play basketball. → We do not (don't) play basketball.
  • 3. He don't study math. → He does not (doesn't) study math.
  • 4. They doesn't have a car. → They do not (don't) have a car.
  • 5. You doesn't know the answer. → You do not (don't) know the answer.
