Unit 7: Forming Questions with Do/Does

Introduction

Objective: By the end of this unit, learners will understand how to form and answer questions using "do" and "does" in the present simple tense.

Overview: This unit introduces the formation of questions with "do" and "does" and provides practice in forming and answering these questions.

Explanation

Forming Questions: To form questions in the present simple tense, use "do" or "does" before the subject and the base form of the verb.

  • Do I/you/we/they work?
  • Does he/she/it work?

Examples

Questions and Answers:

  • Do you like chocolate? Yes, I do.
  • Does she play the piano? No, she doesn't.
  • Do they go to the gym? Yes, they do.
  • Does it snow here? Yes, it does.
  • Do we need more coffee? No, we don't.

Practice Exercises

Exercise 7.1: Form questions using the given prompts:

  • 1. (you / play / tennis) ___ Do you play tennis?
  • 2. (he / work / here) ___ Does he work here?
  • 3. (they / enjoy / music) ___ Do they enjoy music?
  • 4. (she / like / movies) ___ Does she like movies?
  • 5. (you / go / to school) ___ Do you go to school?
  • 6. (it / rain / often) ___ Does it rain often?

Exercise 7.2: Complete the questions using "do" or "does" and the correct form of the verb:

  • 1. ___ (do) you ___ (like) ice cream?
  • 2. ___ (does) she ___ (watch) TV every day?
  • 3. ___ (do) they ___ (play) football on weekends?
  • 4. ___ (does) he ___ (go) to the gym regularly?
  • 5. ___ (do) we ___ (need) more chairs?
  • 6. ___ (does) it ___ (snow) here in winter?

Exercise 7.3: Write questions and short answers:

  • 1. Do you read books? ___ Yes, I do.
  • 2. Does she play the guitar? ___ No, she doesn't.
  • 3. Do they travel often? ___ Yes, they do.
  • 4. Does it get cold here? ___ Yes, it does.
  • 5. Do we have homework today? ___ No, we don't.
  • 6. Does he like pizza? ___ Yes, he does.

Exercise 7.4: Rearrange the words to form questions:

  • 1. (you / where / live / do) ___ Where do you live?
  • 2. (he / what / do / does) ___ What does he do?
  • 3. (they / how / get / to work / do) ___ How do they get to work?
  • 4. (she / when / start / does / work) ___ When does she start work?
  • 5. (you / why / like / do / apples) ___ Why do you like apples?
  • 6. (we / what / eat / do / for breakfast) ___ What do we eat for breakfast?

Exercise 7.5: Write questions for the answers given:

  • 1. Yes, I do. (Do you like coffee?)
  • 2. No, he doesn't. (Does he drive to work?)
  • 3. Yes, they do. (Do they play basketball?)
  • 4. No, it doesn't. (Does it rain here often?)
  • 5. Yes, we do. (Do we have a meeting today?)
  • 6. No, she doesn't. (Does she eat meat?)

Summary

Review: Summarize the key points learned in the unit.

  • Forming questions with "do/does" in the present simple tense.
  • Answering questions in positive and negative forms.
