Our Story

Mustafiz's English Academy was founded with the vision of making English language learning accessible and effective for everyone. With a passion for teaching and years of experience, Mustafiz brings tailored and interactive lessons to learners worldwide.

Our Mission

To empower students by building their confidence and proficiency in English, equipping them with the skills to thrive academically, socially, and professionally.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: We strive to provide high-quality education and resources to ensure student success.
  • Inclusivity: Our courses are designed for all learners, regardless of their background or experience.
  • Community: We foster a supportive learning environment where students can collaborate and grow together.

Meet the Founder

Mustafiz has a deep-rooted passion for teaching and empowering students with language skills that open new doors of opportunity. With almost 7 years of in-person ESL classroom teaching experience since January 2018, he has helped countless learners achieve their language goals.

Mustafiz holds an MSc in Data Science and a BSc in Computer Science & Engineering, blending technical expertise with innovative teaching methods to enhance the learning experience.

IELTS Achievements

Mustafiz's success as a language instructor is backed by his own achievements in the IELTS. In January 2018, he scored an overall band 7 with the following breakdown:

  • Listening: 7.5
  • Speaking: 7
  • Reading: 6.5
  • Writing: 6

In November 2021, he improved his score to an overall band 7.5 with:

  • Listening: 7.5
  • Speaking: 7.5
  • Reading: 8
  • Writing: 7
