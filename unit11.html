Unit 11: Using the Past Tense: worked/got/went

Introduction

Objective: By the end of this unit, learners will understand how to use the past simple tense to describe actions that happened in the past.

Overview: This unit covers the formation and usage of the past simple tense with regular and irregular verbs.

Explanation

Forming the Past Simple: The past simple tense is typically formed by adding -ed to the base form of regular verbs. Irregular verbs have unique past forms.

  • Regular Verbs: play → played, work → worked
  • Irregular Verbs: go → went, have → had

Examples

Positive Statements:

  • I visited my grandparents last weekend.
  • She watched a movie yesterday.
  • They went to the park.
  • He had a big breakfast this morning.

Exercise 11.1: Complete the sentences. Use a verb from the box:

  • arrive, call, decide, forget, invite, leave, return, send, stop, talk
  • 1. I ___ my keys on the table. (left)
  • 2. The package ___ at the post office yesterday. (arrived)
  • 3. She ___ to go for a walk. (decided)
  • 4. They ___ a letter to their friend. (sent)
  • 5. He ___ to his colleague about the project. (talked)
  • 6. We ___ the meeting early. (stopped)
  • 7. She ___ her friend to the party. (invited)
  • 8. They ___ a taxi to the airport. (called)
  • 9. He ___ his wallet at home. (forgot)
  • 10. I ___ from my trip last night. (returned)

Exercise 11.3: Read about John's trip to Paris. Put the verbs in the correct form:

  • Last Monday, John (travel) traveled from New York to Paris.
  • He (wake up) woke up early in the morning and (pack) packed his bags.
  • At 7:00 AM, he (leave) left his apartment and (take) took a taxi to the airport.
  • When he (arrive) arrived, he (check in) checked in and (go) went through security.
  • After that, he (have) had breakfast at a cafe and (wait) waited for his flight.
  • The plane (depart) departed on time and (land) landed in Paris eight hours later.
  • Finally, he (take) took a taxi from the airport to his hotel in the heart of Paris.

  • 4. We frequently (visit) visit our relatives. Last weekend, we (visit) visited our relatives.
  • 5. Mark always (write) writes in his journal. Yesterday, he (write) wrote in his journal.
  • 6. They typically (eat) eat dinner at 7 PM. Last night, they (eat) ate dinner at 7 PM.
  • 7. Jane always (call) calls her mother on Sundays. Last Sunday, she (call) called her mother.
  • 8. Our friends often (travel) travel together. Last month, they (travel) traveled together.
