Unit 72: Expressing Likes, Dislikes, and Interests

Introduction

Objective: By the end of this unit, learners will understand how to express their likes, dislikes, and interests in English.

Overview: This unit focuses on using phrases to convey likes, dislikes, interests, and knowledge about various topics.

Explanation

Expressing Likes and Dislikes: Use phrases such as "I like...", "I don’t like...", "I love...", "I hate...", and "... is/are all right" to express preferences.

  • Example: I like chocolate. I hate exams.

Expressing Interests: Use phrases such as "I'm (very) interested in...", "I'm not interested in...", "I know a lot about...", "I know a little about...", "I don’t know much about...", and "I don’t know anything about..." to talk about interests and knowledge.

  • Example: I'm very interested in history. I don’t know anything about astronomy.

Examples

Likes and Dislikes:

  • I love big cities.
  • I don’t like dogs.
  • Chocolate is all right.

Interests:

  • I'm very interested in history.
  • I know a little about economics.

  • 5. Tennis is all right.

  • 7. I don’t like computer games.

Exercise 72.2: Are you interested in these things? Write sentences with: "I'm (very) interested in...", "I'm not interested in...", "I know a lot about...", "I know a little about...", "I don’t know much about...", "I don’t know anything about..."

  • 1. I'm very interested in history.

  • 3. I'm interested in sport.
  • 4. I don’t know much about art.
  • 5. I don’t know anything about astronomy.
  • 6. I know a little about economics.

Exercise 72.3: Which is right? Complete the sentences

  • 1. My favourite sport is football.
  • 2. I like this hotel. The rooms are very nice.
  • 3. Everybody needs friends.
  • 4. Jane doesn’t go to parties very often.
  • 5. I went shopping this morning. The shops were very busy.
  • 6. 'Where's the milk?' 'It’s in the fridge.'
  • 7. I don’t like milk. I never drink it.
  • 8. 'Do you do any sports?' 'Yes, I play basketball.'
  • 9. 'What does your brother do?' 'He sells computers.'
  • 10. We went for a swim in the river. The water was very cold.
  • 11. I don’t like swimming in cold water.
  • 12. Excuse me, can you pass the salt?
  • 13. I like this town. I like the people here.
  • 14. Vegetables are good for you.
  • 15. The houses in this street are all the same.
  • 16. I can't sing this song. I don’t know the words.
  • 17. I enjoy taking photographs. It's my hobby.
  • 18. Do you want to see the photographs that I took when I was on holiday?
  • 19. English is used a lot in international business.
  • 20. Money doesn’t always bring happiness.

Summary
